Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Basil 1
  • Olive Oil
  • Coarse Salt

Directions

  1. Pile basil leaves on top of one another and roll into a log.
  2. Thinly slice basil into green confetti
  3. Dice tomatoes
  4. Loosely combine all ingredients without bruising them
