Rice Pudding

Luke Taylor

Jul 2, 2023 dessert rice pudding

Servings

--- Servings

Prep time

15 Minutes

Cooking Time

60 Minutes

Calories

--- Kcal

Nice rice pudding using a double-boiler method

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice 12
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) of Eagle brand sweetened condensed milk 3
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Raisins (optional)

Condended Milk Alternative

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup milk

Directions

  1. In a double-boiler 4, bring rice, milk, condensed milk, and salt to a boil
  2. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 1 hour
  3. Add vanilla and 1 tablespoon of butter
    1. Add raisins if desired
  4. Serve warm, top with butter and cinnamon

Condended Milk Alternative

  1. Beat together sugar, egg, cream, and milk (or half and half)
  2. Stir mixture into rice

Footnotes

  1. When cooking aroborio rice, minimize stirring as additional starch is realease when stirred. ↩︎

  2. Other rice may be substituted, but arborio will absorb the milk an dtake on a creamy texture. ↩︎

  3. If not handy, use Condended Milk Alternative ↩︎

  4. Use of a double-boiler will prevent scorching the milk. ↩︎
